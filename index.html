The bhagwat Gita

No doubt, the poetic rendering attracts even many non-believers, let alone the believers. The inner thoughts of it can very easily move one, both a Hindu and a Non-Hindu. And the most significant aspect is that they come from the mouth of Krishna. His speech to Arjuna sounds:Who sees Me everywhere,Who sees the All in Me,For him I am not lost, Nor is he lost for Me

" Yada yada hi dharmasya glaanirbhavati bhaarat

Abhyutthaanamadharmasya tadaatmaanam srijaamyaham

Paritranaay saadhunaam vinaashaay cha dushkritaam

Dharm sansthaapanaarthaay sambhavaami yuge yuge "
